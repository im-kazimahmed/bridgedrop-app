1. Overview

BridgeDrop is a privacy-first, local network file-sharing application. We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring your data stays under your control.

2. Data Collection

BridgeDrop operates on a zero data collection model:

  • No user accounts required
  • No cloud storage or servers
  • No analytics or tracking
  • No personal information collected
  • No data sent to third parties

3. Data Storage (Local Only)

BridgeDrop stores the following data locally on your device:

  • App preferences (theme, download location)
  • Trusted device public keys (for secure pairing)
  • Transfer history (can be cleared)

This data never leaves your device and is not accessible to us or any third party.
