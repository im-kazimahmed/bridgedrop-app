Privacy Policy for BridgeDrop Last Updated: [Current Date] 1. OVERVIEW BridgeDrop is a privacy-first, local network file sharing application. We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring your data stays under your control. 2. DATA COLLECTION BridgeDrop operates on a "zero data collection" model: - NO user accounts required - NO cloud storage or servers - NO analytics or tracking - NO personal information collected - NO data sent to third parties 3. DATA STORAGE (LOCAL ONLY) BridgeDrop stores the following data LOCALLY on your device: - App preferences (theme, download location) - Trusted device public keys (for secure pairing) - Transfer history (stored locally, can be cleared) This data: - Never leaves your device - Is not accessible to us or any third party - Can be deleted by clearing app data 4. PERMISSIONS EXPLAINED - CAMERA: Only used for QR code scanning to pair devices. No photos are stored or transmitted. - STORAGE: Only used to read files you choose to send and save received files. We cannot access other files. - INTERNET/NETWORK: Only used for local network communication between your devices. No internet communication. - LOCATION: NOT used. Network permissions may trigger location permission on some devices, but location is never accessed. 5. DATA TRANSMISSION All file transfers: - Occur only over your LOCAL network (WiFi) - Are end-to-end encrypted (X25519 + AES-GCM) - Never pass through our servers or the internet - Are peer-to-peer between your devices 6. SECURITY - End-to-end encryption for all transfers - Device verification through fingerprint matching - No passwords or accounts to compromise - All cryptographic keys generated and stored locally 7. THIRD-PARTY SERVICES BridgeDrop does NOT use: - Analytics services - Advertising networks - Cloud storage services - Any third-party data collection 8. CHILDREN'S PRIVACY BridgeDrop does not knowingly collect any information from children. The app is suitable for all ages as it collects no personal data. 9. DATA RETENTION - Settings: Stored until app is uninstalled or data is cleared - Transfer history: Stored locally, can be cleared in app settings - Trusted devices: Stored until manually removed or app data cleared 10. YOUR RIGHTS You can: - Clear all app data at any time (Android Settings > Apps > BridgeDrop > Storage > Clear Data) - Revoke permissions at any time (Android Settings > Apps > BridgeDrop > Permissions) - Uninstall the app to remove all data 11. CHANGES TO THIS POLICY We may update this policy. Changes will be posted in the app and on this page. 12. CONTACT US Email: bridgedrop.app@gmail.com Website: https://bridgedrop.kazimahmed.com
